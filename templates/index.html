How It Works

Our advanced system uses machine learning to analyze DNA sequences

1. Upload DNA Sequence

Input your DNA sequence in standard format (A, T, C, G nucleotides). Our system accepts sequences of various lengths for analysis.

2. K-mer Analysis

The sequence is processed using k-mer decomposition and analyzed by our trained RandomForest classifier for pattern recognition.

3. Get Results

Receive probabilistic identity matches with confidence scores, helping you identify potential genetic relationships.
